HOW TO PLAY
1. Search a zone to draw raw materials, not finished rewards.
2. Craft tools and kits, then use them in the matching zone: fish the river, hunt the forest, trap the meadow, signal the high ground.
3. Treat unsafe water, cook or prepare food, and keep hunger, warmth, hydration, and morale above zero.
4. Morale is your action pool each turn.
6. Durable gear moves into stockpile and can be used later.
