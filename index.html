HOW TO PLAY
1. Explore a zone for forage cards, or harvest hard resources like wood, stone, and fire starter.
2. Craft your character's specialty recipes, then use gear like snares, fishing poles, fires, and shelters.
3. Keep hunger, warmth, hydration, and morale above zero while building shared rescue progress.
4. Food and water spoil at end of turn. Durable gear moves into stockpile and can be used later.
